Heading: Most people say "Cow" to refer to all cattle.

Paragraph: By the public a cow is, by definition, a domesticated mammal that is raised for meat and milk. But, further, a male cow is referred to as a bull if it is not castrated and as a steer if it has been castrated.

A female cow that has not yet given birth is known as a heifer, while an adult female that has had at least one calf is referred to as a cow.
